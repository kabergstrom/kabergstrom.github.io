Struggle

2022-04-01

I've struggled to write for a long time. I think it's because I think and worry too much, about whether it'll be good enough and whether the content is appropriate for the reader. Who's The Reader anyway?

I hope to write for myself going forward, and perhaps you, The Reader, will enjoy as a joyous coincidence.
